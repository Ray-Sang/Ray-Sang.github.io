十月的最后一天。

  • lull n. 间歇，暂停，平静期 v. 缓和，使放松，使镇定，使减弱

    • The soft music lulled her to sleep.

      轻柔的音乐使他昏昏欲睡。

    • lullaby 催眠曲，摇篮曲

  • limp v. 跛行；缓慢行进，艰难移动 adj. 软的，柔软的；轻柔的，无力的 n.跛行；艰难缓慢地走

    • His limp is the result of an accident.
    • lame 瘸的，无说服力的
    • limb 树枝，截肢

  • delinquency n. 违法行为；犯罪；不良行为

    • There is a high rate of juvenile delinquency in our city.
    • delinquent adj. 疏忽职务的

  • townscape n. 城镇风光

    • 注意例句：They try to enhance the townscape with attractive landscape and urban design.
    • scape 花茎，引申为美好的风光

  • avail v. 有帮助，有益，有用 n. 益处，效用

    • He tried and tried but all efforts were of no avail.

      他试了又试，但一切努力都不起作用

    • avail oneself of 利用

    • of little/no avail 没有什么用处，没有用

  • essence n. 本质，精髓，要素；香精

    • Speed is of the essence in dealing with an emergency.
    • in enssence 本质上，实质上，基本上
    • be of the essence 极其重要的，必不可少的
    • essential 本质的，尤其重要的，必不可少的，非常重要的

  • session n. 会议；（法庭的）开庭；（进行某活动连续的）一段时间

    • An emergency session was held to cope with this sudden attack.

      将召开一场紧急的会议应对这一突然的袭击。

    • in session 在开会，在开庭

    • summer session 暑期班

  • baron n. 男爵；巨头

    • The baron became increasingly convinced that the letter was written by the young man.
    • duke 公爵；Marquis伯爵；earl子爵；viscount男爵
    • barren adj. 贫瘠的，不孕不育的

  • distort v. 使变形；扭曲，歪曲；曲解

    • The government was accused of having systematically distorted the protestor's case.
    • distort the facts 歪曲事实
    • distort price 操纵价格

  • soak v. 浸泡，浸湿

    • sweat-soaked 汗湿的

  • bind v. 绑，使结合；约束 n. 捆绑物；窘境，困境

    • The suspect bound the hostage's arms with rope and locked him in a room.

      嫌犯用绳子捆住了人质的手臂，并将他锁在一个房间里。

    • bind on 约束，规定

    • bind up 绑起，扎起

  • leukaemia n. 白血病

    • malaria 疟疾
    • anemia 贫血

  • dormant adj. 休眠的，静止的

    • The volcano has remained dormant for several years.
    • a dormant volcano
    • lie dormant 潜伏着

  • fleet adj. 快速的 n. 舰队

    • A fleet of ambulances took the injured to the hospital.
    • a fleet of foot 飞毛腿

  • shrewd adj. 机灵的，聪明的

    • The shrewd businessman made a huge profit by cooperating with a big corporation.

  • mediocre adj. 普通的，一般的，平凡的

    • moderate adj. 适度的，中等的，稳健的； vi.变缓和

  • unyielding adj. 顽强的，不屈的；坚挺的，不易弯曲的

    • The country is unyielding in its demands for a new treaty. 该国坚决要求签订新条约。
    • yield vt. 屈服；出产，放弃 vi. 屈服，投降
    • dauntless adj. 不屈不挠的，大胆的
